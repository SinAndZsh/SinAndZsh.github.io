Ed Tech Is Profitable. It Is Also Mostly Useless

Published: Jan 22, 2026 | Category: Education & Technology | Length: ~1,100 words
文章大意：本文以美国堪萨斯州麦克弗森中学弃用教育软件IXL的真实案例为切入点，结合斯坦福大学等机构的大量独立研究数据，深度剖析了教育技术产业的悖论：虽然全球教育科技市场规模高达1650亿美元，且90%的高中生配备了学校发放的设备，但独立研究表明这些技术很少能真正提升学习效果，甚至往往有害。文章揭示了注意力分散、游戏化过度、人际连接弱化等深层问题，同时承认技术在辅助学习障碍学生和狭窄领域（如拼写、算术）针对性练习方面的有限价值。文章最后呼吁教育界对技术采取"有限、有意识"的使用策略，并引发读者思考：若将数百亿美元投入教师培养而非技术采购，教育将会怎样？

McPherson Middle School in Kansas had been burned/bɜːnd/ v. 烧伤；上当，吃亏（burn的过去分词） before by education technology, but in 2022 school leaders were ready to try again. They selected a digital programme called IXL from a statewide recommendation list. It promised instruction tailored/ˈteɪləd/ adj. 量身定制的 to each student's level, igniting/ɪɡˈnaɪtɪŋ/ v. 点燃；激发（ignite的现在分词） quick gains. The school used it to assign most in-class independent maths work. "We thought it was going to be really magical," says Inge Esping, the principal.

It wasn't. It "didn't really move the needle/muːv ðə ˈniːdl/ 产生实质性影响；带来改变（习语）", Ms Esping says. Students found the programme repetitive/rɪˈpetətɪv/ adj. 重复的；单调的, rigid/ˈrɪdʒɪd/ adj. 僵化的；严格的 and boring — and distraction/dɪˈstrækʃn/ n. 分心；干扰 proved irresistible/ˌɪrɪˈzɪstəbl/ adj. 不可抗拒的 once they were on their school-issued laptops. The school tried blocking YouTube and Spotify, then student-to-student email. But children found workarounds and teachers resented their new surveillance/sɜːˈveɪləns/ n. 监视；监控 duties. In 2025, as parents had long implored/ɪmˈplɔːd/ v. 恳求；哀求（implore的过去分词）, students turned in their laptops, to be brought out only rarely. Pencil and paper now rule; IXL is used sparingly/ˈspeərɪŋli/ adv. 节俭地；少量地, for quick extra practice of maths skills already covered by teachers.

According to researchers, McPherson's experience is a microcosm/ˈmaɪkrəʊkɒzəm/ n. 缩影；微观世界 of the perils/ˈperəlz/ n. 危险；险境（peril的复数） of ed tech. Fifty years after Apple began marketing computers to schools, classrooms are awash/əˈwɒʃ/ adj. 充斥的；泛滥的 with technology. Some 90% of high-school students and 84% of primary-school pupils have school-issued devices; four-fifths of kindergarteners are given them. Concerns about fractured attention and data security are mounting/ˈmaʊntɪŋ/ adj. 不断增加的（mount的现在分词）.

Although ed-tech companies tout huge learning gains, independent research has made clear that technology rarely boosts learning in schools — and often impairs/ɪmˈpeəz/ v. 损害；削弱（impair的第三人称单数） it. A 2024 meta-analysis/ˈmetə əˈnæləsɪs/ n. 元分析（对多项研究结果的综合统计分析方法） of 119 studies of early-literacy tech interventions, led by Rebecca Silverman of Stanford University, found the studies described programmes that delivered at best/æt best/ 充其量；最多 only marginal/ˈmɑːdʒɪnl/ adj. 微小的；边缘的 gains on standardised tests. The majority had little effect, no effect or harmful ones. Jared Horvath, a neuroscientist and author of a book called "The Digital Delusion", has reviewed meta-analyses covering tens of thousands of studies. His verdict/ˈvɜːdɪkt/ n. 结论；裁决: "In nearly every context, ed tech doesn't come close to the minimum threshold/ˈθreʃhəʊld/ n. 门槛；阈值；临界点 for meaningful learning impact."

The prevalence of tech in schools owes/əʊz/ v. 归功于；归因于（owe的第三人称单数） less to rigorous/ˈrɪɡərəs/ adj. 严格的；严谨的 evidence than aggressive/əˈɡresɪv/ adj. 激进的；咄咄逼人的；有闯劲的 marketing. Teachers are now flooded with daily offers for free tech. In 2024 American schools spent $30bn on education technology. Globally, it is a $165bn industry. Technology does save money on textbooks and streamline/ˈstriːmlaɪn/ v. 使高效；简化 lesson planning. But licensing and training costs add up, and many teachers feel burdened/ˈbɜːdnd/ adj. 负担沉重的（burden的过去分词） rather than liberated/ˈlɪbəreɪtɪd/ adj. 解放的；自由的（liberate的过去分词） by all the admin and dashboards.

Long-term trends raise the possibility that the rise of in-class devices is responsible for/rɪˈspɒnsəbl fɔː/ 对……负责；是……的原因 an alarming/əˈlɑːmɪŋ/ adj. 令人担忧的 decline in performance in reading and other subjects. Scores on 21 nationwide benchmark/ˈbentʃmɑːk/ n. 基准；标杆 tests rose from 1994 until peaking in 2012-15, when screen use started to soar; they then began to sink. In major assessments for maths, science and reading from 2011 to 2019, greater in-school computer use for learning correlates/ˈkɒrəleɪts/ v. 相关；关联（correlate的第三人称单数） with lower scores. In contrast, students in classes with rare or no computer use at all typically score highest.

Distraction is one likely culprit/ˈkʌlprɪt/ n. 罪魁祸首；元凶. Another is that some tools emphasise gamification/ˌɡeɪmɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ n. 游戏化 at the expense of/æt ðɪ ɪkˈspens ɒv/ 以……为代价 education, meaning that children focus on/ˈfəʊkəs ɒn/ 专注于 winning points than mastering concepts. But there are more insidious/ɪnˈsɪdiəs/ adj. 阴险的；潜伏的；暗中为害的 issues, such as the ways digital tools weaken human connection and empathy/ˈempəθi/ n. 共情；同理心 in the classroom.

Evidence shows that apps can support learning through drills/drɪlz/ n. 操练；练习（drill的复数） in two areas: for certain learning disabilities/ˌdɪsəˈbɪlətiz/ n. 残疾；障碍（disability的复数） and in adaptive tutoring in narrow domains where there are clear right and wrong answers, such as spelling and arithmetic. But although students may improve through repetition "within the game", they struggle to/ˈstrʌɡl tuː/ 努力做；挣扎着做 transfer knowledge to other contexts such as standardised tests.

Common sense argues for differentiating/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃieɪtɪŋ/ v. 区分；区别（differentiate的现在分词） between age cohorts/ˈkəʊhɔːts/ n. 一群人；同辈；同期群（cohort的复数） when it comes to tech. "Particularly for younger children, what's most important is that they are interacting with other humans," says Jeffrey Greene of the University of North Carolina. For older ages, Ms Esping and Rodney Trice, a North Carolina district superintendent, advocate "limited, intentional/ɪnˈtenʃənl/ adj. 有意的；故意的" use. "The pendulum/ˈpendjələm/ n. 钟摆；（喻）摇摆不定的事态 has swung toward devices determining the assignment rather than the other way around," Mr Trice says.

Back in 2013, Bill Gates remarked that it would take a decade to know whether education technology really worked. More than ten years and hundreds of billions of dollars later, the answer is increasingly clear. Notes Emily Cherkin, an advocate/ˈædvəkeɪt/ n. 倡导者；拥护者 and fed-up parent: "Imagine if all that money had gone into teachers instead."

长难句解析 Sentence Analysis

Sentence 1: A 2024 meta-analysis of 119 studies of early-literacy tech interventions, led by Rebecca Silverman of Stanford University, found the studies described programmes that delivered at best only marginal gains on standardised tests.
【参考译文】斯坦福大学Rebecca Silverman主导的2024年一项元分析，综合了119项早期识字技术干预研究，发现这些研究所描述的项目充其量只能在标准化测试中带来微小的提升。
【结构分析】主句主语为A 2024 meta-analysis，后跟两个of介词短语作后置定语（of 119 studies / of early-literacy tech interventions）。led by...为过去分词短语作后置定语修饰meta-analysis。谓语动词found后接省略that的宾语从句。宾语从句中，programmes后接that引导的定语从句，其中at best为插入语，意为"充其量"。注意deliver gains为"带来提升"的学术表达。
Sentence 2: Long-term trends raise the possibility that the rise of in-class devices is responsible for an alarming decline in performance in reading and other subjects.
【参考译文】长期趋势引发了一种可能性：课堂设备的普及正是阅读及其他学科成绩令人担忧地下降的原因。
【结构分析】主句为Long-term trends raise the possibility，that引导同位语从句解释possibility的具体内容。同位语从句中，主语为the rise of in-class devices，谓语为is responsible for，宾语为an alarming decline，后接in performance...作后置定语。"be responsible for"在此处表示"是……的原因"，是考研英语中常见的因果表达。
Sentence 3: But although students may improve through repetition "within the game", they struggle to transfer knowledge to other contexts such as standardised tests.
【参考译文】但是，尽管学生可能通过"游戏内"的重复练习取得进步，他们却很难将知识迁移到标准化测试等其他情境中。
【结构分析】although引导让步状语从句，主句为they struggle to transfer knowledge。"struggle to do sth."表示"努力/挣扎着做某事"，隐含"难以做到"之意。transfer...to...为"将……迁移到……"，是认知心理学和教育学中的核心概念。such as引导举例，说明other contexts的具体内容。

阅读理解练习 Reading Comprehension

1. 主旨大意题：What is the main idea of the passage?
正确答案：B
【解析】文章以麦克弗森中学弃用IXL软件的实例开篇，随后引用大量独立研究数据论证主旨：尽管教育科技产业规模高达1650亿美元，且学校设备普及率极高，但独立研究表明这些技术很少能提升学习效果，甚至在多数情况下有害。A项与文意相反（文中说营销非常激进）；C项过于极端（文章并未主张"立即禁止所有设备"，而是呼吁"有限、有意识"的使用）；D项片面（成本只是次要问题，核心问题是学习效果）。
2. 细节理解题：According to the passage, in which area can educational apps genuinely support learning?
正确答案：B
【解析】根据文章倒数第三段，"Evidence shows that apps can support learning through drills in two areas: for certain learning disabilities and in adaptive tutoring in narrow domains where there are clear right and wrong answers, such as spelling and arithmetic." 明确指出技术能在两个领域发挥作用：一是辅助某些学习障碍的学生，二是在拼写、算术等有明确对错的狭窄领域进行适应性辅导。A、C、D三项在文中均无依据。
3. 推理判断题：What can be inferred from Bill Gates' remark and Emily Cherkin's comment at the end of the passage?
正确答案：B
【解析】文章结尾引用比尔·盖茨2013年的话（需要十年才能知道教育技术是否有效），以及十多年后家长Emily Cherkin的感慨："Imagine if all that money had gone into teachers instead." 结合前文"hundreds of billions of dollars later, the answer is increasingly clear"，可以推断出作者暗示这些巨额资金若投入教师培养而非技术采购，可能会产生更好的教育效果。A项过于极端；C项与文意相反（Gates只是说要等十年，而结果表明效果不佳）；D项错误（文中提到parents had long implored学生上交笔记本电脑）。

阅读技巧提示

  • 议论文常采用"具体案例 → 数据论证 → 深层分析 → 结论建议"的结构。阅读时注意把握论证脉络，案例往往服务于主旨。
  • 注意文中表示对比关系的结构，如"less to... than..."（与其说……不如说……）、"rather than"（而不是），这类结构往往是作者观点的集中体现。
  • 引语和直接引语在文章中通常用于增强论证的可信度，引用专家或当事人的话往往是细节题和推理题的出题点。
  • 文末的总结性语句（如本文最后一段）常常点明或深化文章主旨，是解答主旨大意题的关键区域。
  • 遇到学术词汇如meta-analysis（元分析）、correlate（相关）、threshold（阈值）时，不要慌张，通过后文解释或同位语往往可以推测其大致含义。
