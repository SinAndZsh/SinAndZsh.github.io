Oceans Under 'Severe and Accelerating' Pressure, UN Assessment Finds

Published: June 30, 2026 | Category: Environment & Science | Length: ~1,280 words
文章大意：联合国发布第三次世界海洋评估报告，由来自86个国家的约600名科学家历时五年编撰完成。报告指出全球海洋正面临"严重且不断加剧"的压力：海水变暖、酸化、塑料污染（每年5210万吨塑料垃圾入海）、过度捕捞等问题相互叠加，威胁着海洋生态系统和数十亿人的生计。报告同时强调，尽管形势严峻，但通过减少污染、建立海洋保护区、可持续渔业管理等手段，人类仍有机会扭转部分损害。本文围绕这一评估的核心发现、对人类社会的影响以及可能的解决路径展开深度报道。

The world's oceans are under severe/sɪˈvɪə(r)/ adj. 严重的；严峻的 and accelerating/əkˈseləreɪtɪŋ/ adj. 加速的；不断加剧的 pressure from human activity, according to a landmark/ˈlændmɑːk/ n./adj. 里程碑；有重大意义的 United Nations assessment released this week. The report, the third of its kind and the most comprehensive/ˌkɒmprɪˈhensɪv/ adj. 全面的；综合的 to date, warns that compounding/ˈkɒmpaʊndɪŋ/ adj. 复合的；叠加的 threats are pushing marine ecosystems toward irreversible/ɪˈrɪvɜːsəbl/ adj. 不可逆的；无法挽回的 damage.

Compiled over five years by approximately 600 experts from 86 countries, the 1,352-page assessment paints a grim/ɡrɪm/ adj. 严峻的；令人沮丧的 picture of the state of the world's oceans. "The science is unequivocal/ˌʌnɪˈkwɪvəkl/ adj. 明确的；毫不含糊的," said one of the report's coordinating authors. "Human activities are changing the ocean at a pace and scale unprecedented/ʌnˈpresɪdentɪd/ adj. 前所未有的；史无前例的 in Earth's history."

Among the most alarming findings is the magnitude/ˈmæɡnɪtjuːd/ n. 巨大；重要性；震级 of plastic pollution. The assessment estimates that 52.1 million tonnes of plastic waste enter the ocean each year, contributing to an estimated 24.4 trillion microplastic particles dispersed/dɪˈspɜːst/ adj. 分散的；散布的 throughout marine environments. These particles have been detected in the deepest ocean trenches/ˈtrentʃɪz/ n. 海沟；战壕（trench复数） and inside the bodies of organisms/ˈɔːɡənɪzəmz/ n. 生物体；有机体（organism复数） at every level of the food chain.

Ocean warming represents another existential/ˌeɡzɪˈstenʃl/ adj. 存在性的；关乎生存的 threat. Since the Industrial Revolution, the ocean has absorbed more than 90 percent of the excess heat trapped by greenhouse gases. This warming is disrupting/dɪsˈrʌptɪŋ/ v. 扰乱；使中断（disrupt的现在分词） migration patterns, bleaching/ˈbliːtʃɪŋ/ n./adj. 白化；漂白 coral reefs, and intensifying/ɪnˈtensɪfaɪɪŋ/ v. 加剧；增强（intensify的现在分词） the destructive power of tropical storms. Marine heatwaves — periods of abnormally/æbˈnɔːməli/ adv. 异常地；反常地 high ocean temperatures — have doubled in frequency since the 1980s.

The assessment also documents the cumulative/ˈkjuːmjələtɪv/ adj. 累积的；渐增的 effects of multiple stressors. Agricultural runoff, sewage, chemical contamination, and oil spills interact with warming and acidification to create what scientists call "synergistic/ˌsɪnəˈdʒɪstɪk/ adj. 协同的；协作增效的 damage" — harm greater than the sum of its parts. Coastal ecosystems such as mangrove forests and seagrass meadows, which serve as natural buffers/ˈbʌfəz/ n. 缓冲物；缓冲区（buffer复数） against storm surges, are deteriorating/dɪˈtɪəriəreɪtɪŋ/ v. 恶化；退化（deteriorate的现在分词） at an alarming rate.

For human communities, the consequences are deeply intertwined/ˌɪntəˈtwaɪnd/ adj. 紧密交织的；缠绕的 with economic and social stability. Approximately 3 billion people depend on marine and coastal biodiversity for their livelihoods. Fish provide more than 20 percent of the animal protein consumed by low-income countries. As fish stocks deplete/dɪˈpliːt/ v. 耗尽；使枯竭 and ocean productivity declines, food security for the world's most vulnerable/ˈvʌlnərəbl/ adj. 脆弱的；易受伤害的 populations grows increasingly precarious/prɪˈkeəriəs/ adj. 不稳定的；危险的.

The report identifies a significant "governance gap" as a root cause of ocean degradation. Despite hundreds of international agreements, enforcement/ɪnˈfɔːsmənt/ n. 执行；实施 remains weak, and coordination between nations is fragmented/ˈfræɡmentɪd/ adj. 碎片化的；分裂的. Nearly two-thirds of the ocean lies beyond national jurisdiction, creating what some experts call a "tragedy of the commons" where no single nation bears responsibility for stewardship/ˈstjuːədʃɪp/ n. 管理；监护； stewardship.

Yet the assessment is not without hope. The authors emphasize that the ocean possesses remarkable resilience/rɪˈzɪliəns/ n. 恢复力；韧性 if given a chance to recover. They outline several pathways toward sustainability: expanding marine protected areas, transitioning/trænˈzɪʃənɪŋ/ v. 转变；过渡（transition的现在分词） to sustainable fishing practices, reducing plastic production at the source, and decarbonizing/diːˈkɑːbənaɪzɪŋ/ v. 脱碳；减少碳排放（decarbonize的现在分词） maritime industries. The report notes that when pollution is reduced and fishing pressure eased, marine populations can rebound surprisingly quickly.

The question, scientists say, is whether humanity can marshal/ˈmɑːʃl/ v. 集结；组织；调动 the political will and financial resources to act before tipping points/ˈtɪpɪŋ pɔɪnts/ n. 临界点； tipping point复数 are crossed. "We know what needs to be done," one marine biologist said. "What we lack is the urgency to do it at the scale required." The assessment makes clear that the next decade will be decisive/dɪˈsaɪsɪv/ adj. 决定性的；关键的 in determining whether future generations inherit a thriving ocean or a diminished/dɪˈmɪnɪʃt/ adj. 减少的；削弱的 one.

长难句解析 Sentence Analysis

Sentence 1: The assessment estimates that 52.1 million tonnes of plastic waste enter the ocean each year, contributing to an estimated 24.4 trillion microplastic particles dispersed throughout marine environments.
【结构分析】主句主语为The assessment，谓语为estimates，后接that引导的宾语从句。宾语从句中，"contributing to..."为现在分词短语作结果状语，表示前文动作带来的结果。"dispersed throughout marine environments"为过去分词短语作后置定语，修饰microplastic particles。
Sentence 2: Agricultural runoff, sewage, chemical contamination, and oil spills interact with warming and acidification to create what scientists call "synergistic damage" — harm greater than the sum of its parts.
【结构分析】主语为四个并列名词短语（Agricultural runoff, sewage, chemical contamination, and oil spills），谓语为interact with，宾语为warming and acidification。不定式短语"to create..."作目的状语。what引导的宾语从句作create的宾语。破折号后为harm的同位语，后接形容词短语"greater than..."作后置定语。
Sentence 3: The assessment makes clear that the next decade will be decisive in determining whether future generations inherit a thriving ocean or a diminished one.
【结构分析】主句为"The assessment makes clear that..."，其中clear为宾语补足语，that引导的从句为真正的宾语（宾语后置）。that从句中，"in determining..."为介词短语作状语。determining后接whether引导的宾语从句，从句中使用了"whether...or..."结构，表示"是……还是……"。

阅读理解 Reading Comprehension

1. 主旨大意题：What is the main idea of the passage?
正确答案：B
【解析】文章围绕联合国第三次世界海洋评估报告展开，先指出海洋面临的严峻威胁（变暖、酸化、塑料污染、过度捕捞等），再强调这些威胁对人类社会的深远影响，最后提出海洋具有恢复力、若采取行动仍可扭转局面的希望。A项过度否定（文章并未说UN失败）；C项以偏概全且过于绝对（文章未提"所有珊瑚礁将在2030年前毁灭"）；D项片面（塑料污染只是众多威胁之一）。
2. 推理判断题：What can be inferred from the passage about the governance of oceans?
正确答案：B
【解析】文章第七段明确指出，尽管存在数百项国际协议，但"enforcement remains weak, and coordination between nations is fragmented"（执行力度薄弱，各国之间的协调是碎片化的），且近三分之二的海域超出国家管辖范围，导致"公地悲剧"。这直接支持B选项。A项与原文相反；C项与原文矛盾（大部分海域在国家管辖之外）；D项无依据。
3. 词义猜测题：In paragraph 5, the word "synergistic" most probably means ______.
正确答案：B
【解析】原文中"synergistic damage"后的破折号明确解释："harm greater than the sum of its parts"（伤害大于各部分之和）。这符合"协同效应"的概念——多种压力因素相互作用，产生的整体破坏力超过单一因素破坏力的简单相加。A项"相互抵消"与原文相反；C项"单一来源"与synergistic的"协同"含义矛盾；D项"易于逆转"在文中无依据。

  • "not without hope"等双重否定结构是作者态度的转折点，常暗示后文将提出解决方案或积极展望。
