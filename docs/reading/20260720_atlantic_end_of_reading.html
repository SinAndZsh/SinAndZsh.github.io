The End of Reading Is Here

阅读技巧提示

  • 抓主旨：本文是典型的问题-分析-影响型议论文，首段通过类比引出核心论点，注意把握"postliterate"这一核心概念

  • 猜词义：遇到生词先通过上下文逻辑和词根词缀推测，如"postliterate" = post(后) + literate(识字的)

✂️ 长难句解析

1. The fact that the library was allowed to die showed that the dark age had already arrived.
结构分析：主语 The fact + 同位语从句 that the library was allowed to die + 谓语 showed + 宾语从句 that the dark age had already arrived。
翻译：图书馆被任由消亡这一事实表明，黑暗时代已经来临。
2. What has changed is what they read, and how.
结构分析：What 引导主语从句 + is + what 引导表语从句 + and how (省略结构，完整为 how they read)。
翻译：改变的并非阅读量，而是他们读什么、以及如何读。
考点提示：注意第二个 what 和 how 均引导名词性从句，这种"What... is what..."结构是考研英语常见强调句型。
3. Writing detaches the message from the messenger, allowing for a more dispassionate spread of information than was possible in oral societies.
结构分析：主语 Writing + 谓语 detaches + 宾语 the message + from 介词短语 + 现在分词 allowing for... 作结果状语 + than 引导比较状语从句（省略了 it）。
翻译：文字将信息与传递者分离，使得信息传播比口语社会可能的传播方式更加冷静客观。
4. By asking them to read, professors are arbitrarily withholding information from students by forcing them to get it through this more difficult medium.
结构分析：By asking... 方式状语 + 主语 professors + 谓语 are withholding + 宾语 information + from students + by forcing... 方式状语（内含 force sb. to do sth. 结构）。
翻译：通过要求他们阅读，教授们正在武断地向学生隐瞒信息，强迫他们通过这种更困难的媒介获取信息。
考点提示：注意引语中使用了反讽修辞，这是学生抱怨阅读的转述，并非作者观点。

📝 阅读理解练习

1. 主旨大意题 What is the main idea of the passage?

A. American society is transitioning from literacy to post-literacy, where people can read words but lose deep comprehension abilities.
B. The Library of Alexandria was destroyed by war and fire, which led to the Dark Ages in human history.
C. Modern technology has increased the amount of reading Americans do every day.
D. Harvard students are struggling to read classical literature due to poor education quality.
正确答案：A
文章以亚历山大图书馆的衰落引入，随后用大量数据说明美国阅读量下降、阅读能力退化，并提出"postliterate"（后文字时代）这一核心概念——人们虽能识字但丧失深度阅读能力。B选项只涉及引入部分；C与原文相反；D只是文中一个细节例证。

2. 推理判断题 What can be inferred from the passage about the author's attitude toward the decline of reading?

A. The author believes it is a natural evolution of communication technology, similar to the decline of radio listening.
B. The author views the decline of reading as a profound civilizational crisis that affects consciousness, politics, and culture.
C. The author thinks that watching news videos is just as effective as reading newspapers for civic engagement.
D. The author suggests that schools should assign fewer books to accommodate students' diminished reading levels.
正确答案：B
从文章最后几段可以看出，作者将阅读衰落的后果提升到文明层面："The decline of reading will bring about changes of the same magnitude. It will affect our innermost thoughts, our society's politics and culture..." 同时引用Walter Ong和Neil Postman的论述强调阅读对人类意识的塑造作用。A是作者明确反对的观点（通过引用Cal Newport的论述间接反驳）；C、D均与作者立场相反。

3. 词义猜测题 The word "postliterate" in Paragraph 6 is closest in meaning to ________.

A. completely unable to read or write
B. having advanced literary skills beyond ordinary people
C. able to decode words but losing higher-order comprehension and synthesis abilities
D. preferring oral communication over written language since childhood
正确答案：C
定位第六段："That doesn't mean they are forgetting how to decode individual words. Rather, they are losing the higher-order abilities of comprehension and synthesis. America, in other words, isn't illiterate. It's postliterate." 由此可知，postliterate不是illiterate（文盲），而是能解码单词但丧失了高阶理解和综合能力。A是illiterate的含义；B、D文中均未提及。

📋 文章大意总结

本文是The Atlantic 2026年7月封面文章，作者Rose Horowitch通过类比亚历山大图书馆因"疏忽"而消亡的历史，引出当代美国社会面临的阅读危机。文章引用大量调查数据指出：美国人阅读量持续下降，阅读能力（尤其是深层理解和推理能力）严重退化，青少年阅读兴趣断崖式下跌。作者提出"postliterate"（后文字时代）这一核心概念——人们并非不识字，而是丧失了深度阅读和思考的能力。

文章进一步从认知科学和历史角度论证阅读对人类意识、逻辑推理、科学发展和民主政治的奠基性作用，引用Walter Ong、Neil Postman、本杰明·富兰克林等人的观点，强调阅读衰落将带来文明级别的深远影响。文章适合考研英语一/二阅读训练，涵盖教育、科技与社会多个交叉话题，数据密集、论证严密，是练习主旨题、推理题和词义猜测题的上佳材料。
