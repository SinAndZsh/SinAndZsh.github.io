"This shift is driven by AI's dual capability to write code and exploit vulnerabilities at a scale and speed previously impossible for human hackers alone."
【句子结构】主语(This shift) + 被动谓语(is driven by) + 宾语(AI's dual capability) + 不定式修饰(to write... and exploit...) + 状语(at a scale and speed) + 后置定语(previously impossible...)

【词汇点拨】dual capability 双重能力；previously impossible 之前不可能的

【参考译文】这一转变是由人工智能的双重能力所推动的——它既能编写代码，又能以人类黑客单独无法达到的规模和速度利用漏洞。
"According to Moody's Ratings, the time attackers take to exploit a public vulnerability dropped from over 700 days in 2020 to just 44 days in 2025."
【句子结构】状语(According to...) + 主语(the time) + 定语从句(attackers take to exploit...) + 谓语(dropped) + 状语(from... to...)

【语法要点】attackers take to exploit... 是省略了关系代词that的定语从句，修饰the time

【参考译文】据穆迪评级机构称，攻击者利用公开漏洞所需的时间从2020年的700多天锐减至2025年的仅44天。
"A compounding issue is the rise of 'vibe-coding,' where humans rely on AI agents to write software without rigorous verification, frequently producing insecure code that introduces new vulnerabilities even as old ones are patched."
【句子结构】主语(A compounding issue) + 系动词(is) + 表语(the rise...) + 非限制性定语从句(where...) + 现在分词短语(producing...) + 定语从句(that...) + 时间状语从句(even as...)

📚 阅读理解练习
主旨大意 1. What is the main idea of the passage?
A. AI technology has made software development more efficient but also more dangerous.
B. Traditional cybersecurity methods are completely useless in the age of AI.
C. The rise of AI has fundamentally changed the cybersecurity landscape, making breaches inevitable and requiring a new defensive mindset.
D. Government should ban advanced AI models to protect national security.
正确答案：C
【解析】文章开篇即指出互联网安全模式因AI而"fundamentally collapsed"，核心论点是人们必须从"假设不会被黑"转向"假设一定会被黑"，并在末段强调这是一场"fundamental shift"。A选项只提到软件开发，未触及核心论点；B选项"completely useless"过于绝对；D选项的"ban advanced AI models"并非文章主旨。
细节理解 2. According to the article, what happened to the time required to exploit a public vulnerability?
A. It increased from 44 days to over 700 days due to better security measures.
B. It dropped from over 700 days in 2020 to just 44 days in 2025.
C. It remained stable at around 400 days between 2020 and 2025.
D. It fluctuated dramatically depending on the type of vulnerability.
正确答案：B
【解析】根据文章第四段："the time attackers take to exploit a public vulnerability dropped from over 700 days in 2020 to just 44 days in 2025"，攻击者利用公开漏洞的时间从2020年的700多天大幅缩短至2025年的仅44天。注意题目问的是"exploit a vulnerability"（攻击者利用漏洞的时间），而非修补漏洞的时间。
推理判断 3. What can be inferred about the author's attitude toward revoking public access to Claude Mythos?
A. The author fully supports the government's decision to restrict access for national security.
B. The author believes it is a necessary step to prevent AI from falling into the wrong hands.
C. The author is critical of the move, suggesting it removes a vital defense tool from the private sector.
D. The author remains completely neutral and offers no opinion on the matter.
正确答案：C
【解析】文章第七段明确指出，政府迫使Anthropic撤销公开访问是"a move critics describe as removing a vital cyberdefense tool from the private sector while open-source alternatives continue to evolve"。作者引用批评者的观点，暗示这种做法实际上是削弱了私营部门的防御能力，而开源替代方案仍在继续发展。由此可推断作者对此举持批评态度。A和B与文意相反，D不符合文章语气。
💡 阅读技巧提示

考研英语阅读技巧

  • 关注转折词：遇到but, however, yet等转折词时，重点往往在转折之后，常设考点。
  • 识别绝对化表达：选项中出现completely, never, always等绝对化词汇时，往往是干扰项（如第1题B选项）。
  • 长难句抓主干：遇到复杂句子先找主谓宾，忽略修饰成分，理解核心意思后再逐层添加细节。
  • 数据定位法：题干中出现数字、年份、人名时，可快速回原文定位，提高做题效率。
  • 态度题看措辞：注意作者使用的形容词和副词的情感色彩，如vital, crucial等正面词 vs. futile, disastrous等负面词。
