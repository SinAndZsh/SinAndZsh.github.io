Britain to Ban Social Media for Under-16s in World-Leading Child Safety Push

France, Indonesia and Spain have all moved to restrict access for those under 15 or 16, while Ireland is working with European Union partners on age-verification standards.

Ministers insist the decision is a direct response to mounting evidence that heavy social-media use can harm adolescent mental health. "We are going further than any country in the world by banning social media for under-16s and putting wider protections in place to give kids their childhood back," Starmer said at the launch. Such proposals, however, are harder to draft into law and more expensive to enforce.

Public opinion appears broadly supportive. Polling conducted after the announcement suggests that roughly two-thirds of British adults back the ban, with parents of teenagers among the most enthusiastic. Among young people themselves, surveys paint a more mixed picture: many report that social media is essential to their social lives and sense of belonging, while also recognising its negative effects on mood and self-esteem.

文章大意

本文选自《卫报》2026年7月社会/科技版块，聚焦英国政府计划禁止16岁以下儿童使用主流社交媒体这一重大政策。首相基尔·斯塔默宣布相关立法将在圣诞节前提交议会，预计2027年春季生效，TikTok、Instagram、YouTube、Snapchat、Facebook 和 X 等平台将纳入禁令范围，而 WhatsApp、Signal 及 YouTube Kids 等可获得豁免。

文章呈现了支持与反对两种声音：支持者包括家长、教师和心理健康倡导者，他们认为大量研究已将青少年重度使用社交媒体与焦虑、抑郁、睡眠恶化等问题相关联；反对者则担忧一刀切禁令可能被青少年用 VPN 或借用账户规避，反而将脆弱儿童推向监管更弱的网络角落，同时引发隐私、数字排斥和执行成本等问题。

文章最后指出，这一政策的成败将成为全球民主国家监管青少年网络空间的试金石：若成功，可能成为他国模板；若失败，则可能强化“教育与监督优于禁止”的立场。整体而言，文章以平衡的视角探讨了儿童数字权利与保护之间的张力。

阅读技巧提示

  • 把握政策类文章结构：本文是典型的“政策宣布—实施细则—各方反应—潜在挑战—国际影响”结构。阅读时先定位政府动作，再梳理支持/反对观点，最后关注结论句，可快速抓住全文脉络。
  • 注意同义替换：考研阅读常将原文词汇替换为选项中的同义表达。例如文中的 prohibit、ban、restrict access 可相互对应；advocates、supporters、campaigners 表达相近立场。
  • 识别让步与转折：Yet、However、While 等转折词后常出现作者或反对者观点，是细节题和推理题的高频考点。本文第二段介绍政策后，第五段以 Yet 引出批评声音，需特别留意。
  • 关注结尾的“如果……”结构：文章末段使用 If...it could...; if...it may... 的对比句式，常用于推断题，提示政策的不确定性和全球示范意义。

长难句解析

The UK government has announced plans to prohibit mainstream social-media platforms from offering their services to children under 16, a move ministers describe as the most ambitious attempt anywhere in the world to protect young people from the proliferation of online harms.
主干为 "The UK government has announced plans"。不定式 "to prohibit..." 作后置定语修饰 plans；"a move ministers describe as..." 为同位语，补充说明前文整项计划，其中 "ministers describe as" 为省略了关系代词的定语从句，修饰 move；"to protect young people..." 为不定式作后置定语修饰 attempt。
They warn that a blanket ban could be circumvented by tech-savvy teenagers using virtual private networks or borrowed accounts, while inadvertently driving more vulnerable children toward unregulated corners of the internet where oversight is weaker.
主干为 "They warn that..."，that 引导宾语从句。从句中 "a blanket ban could be circumvented" 为被动语态；"using virtual private networks or borrowed accounts" 为现在分词短语作方式状语；"while inadvertently driving..." 为省略结构，相当于 "while it is inadvertently driving..."；"where oversight is weaker" 为定语从句修饰 corners。
If the UK scheme succeeds, it could become a template for other democracies; if it fails, it may strengthen the hand of those who argue for education and oversight over prohibition.
本句为两个并列的条件句，用分号连接。前一分句中 "If the UK scheme succeeds" 为条件状语从句，主句 "it could become a template for other democracies"；后一分句结构相同。"who argue for education and oversight over prohibition" 为定语从句修饰 those，说明支持“教育与监督而非禁止”立场的人群。

阅读理解练习

主旨大意题
1. What is the passage mainly about?
A) The history of social media regulation in the European Union.
B) The UK government's plan to ban social media for children under 16 and the surrounding debate.
C) A comparison of mental-health treatments for adolescents in Britain and Australia.
D) The technical details of age-verification systems used by social-media platforms.
正确答案：B
解析：文章开篇即点明英国政府计划禁止16岁以下儿童使用主流社交媒体，随后介绍覆盖范围、豁免对象、支持理由、批评声音、执行挑战及国际影响。B 项准确概括全文主旨；A、C、D 三项均为局部细节或偏离主题。
细节理解题
2. According to the passage, which of the following is likely to be exempt from the ban?
A) TikTok and Instagram.
B) Snapchat and Facebook.
C) WhatsApp and YouTube Kids.
D) X and YouTube.
正确答案：C
解析：文章第二段明确指出，"Messaging services such as WhatsApp and Signal, as well as the YouTube Kids app, are expected to be exempt"，因此 C 项正确。A、B、D 中的平台均在禁令范围内。
推理判断题
3. What can be inferred about the critics' attitude toward the proposed ban?
A) They believe the ban will completely eliminate online harms for teenagers.
B) They think the ban is too lenient and should apply to users under 18.
C) They doubt its practical effectiveness and worry about unintended negative consequences.
D) They argue that social media has no connection to adolescent mental-health problems.
正确答案：C
解析：批评者认为一刀切禁令可能被懂技术的青少年规避（circumvented），同时可能 inadvertently 将脆弱儿童推向监管更弱的网络角落，还可能因身份验证要求而使弱势家庭边缘化。这些都体现了他们对执行效果和意外后果的担忧，故 C 项正确。
