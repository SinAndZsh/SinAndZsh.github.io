Ed Tech is Profitable. It Is Also Mostly Useless

来源：The Economist  |  日期：2026-01-22  |  话题：教育 / 科技
字数：约 1,200 词  |  难度：★★★★☆

📋 文章大意

本文选自《经济学人》，探讨了教育科技（Ed Tech）在课堂中的实际效果。文章以堪萨斯州麦克弗森中学使用数字教学项目失败的案例为引子，指出尽管教育科技行业利润丰厚，但独立研究表明技术很少能真正提升学习效果，甚至常常损害学习。文章分析了成绩下降与屏幕使用时间增加的相关性，讨论了分心、游戏化等问题，并指出只有在特定 narrow 领域（如拼写和算术）中，教育科技才可能提供有限的帮助。文章最后反思，如果巨额资金投入到教师身上而非技术设备，效果或许会更好。

🎯 阅读技巧提示

  • 关注转折词： 本文出现大量转折（but, although, however），这些往往是作者真正观点所在，考研阅读中常在转折处设题。
  • 例证题思路： 文中McPherson中学的案例是典型的"论点+例证"结构，阅读时注意区分例子本身和例子支撑的论点。
  • 因果逻辑： 注意区分"correlate with"（相关）和"responsible for"（导致），文中对成绩下降的原因分析是重点。
  • 作者态度： 通过形容词和副词判断态度，如"mostly useless""insidious""alarming"等词表明作者对教育科技持批判态度。

🔍 长难句解析

A 2024 meta-analysis of 119 studies of early-literacy tech interventions, led by Rebecca Silverman of Stanford University, found the studies described programmes that delivered at best only marginal gains on standardised tests.
结构分析：
主语：A 2024 meta-analysis（2024年的一项元分析）
后置定语1：of 119 studies of early-literacy tech interventions（对119项早期识字技术干预研究的）
后置定语2：led by Rebecca Silverman...（由斯坦福大学Rebecca Silverman主导的）
谓语：found（发现）
宾语从句：the studies described programmes that...（这些研究描述的项目至多只在标准化测试中取得了边际收益）
考点： 多重后置定语嵌套，that引导定语从句修饰programmes。
Scores on 21 nationwide benchmark tests rose from 1994 until peaking in 2012-15, when screen use started to soar; they then began to sink.
结构分析：
分号连接两个并列句。前句主语：Scores on 21 nationwide benchmark tests；谓语：rose；until peaking... 为时间状语；when引导非限制性定语从句修饰2012-15。后句主语they指代scores，谓语began to sink。
考点： when引导的定语从句修饰时间点；分号连接并列句表示对比转折。
But although students may improve through repetition "within the game", they struggle to transfer knowledge to other contexts such as standardised tests.
结构分析：
Although引导让步状语从句（虽然学生可能通过"游戏内"的重复练习取得进步），主句为they struggle to transfer knowledge...（他们难以将知识迁移到标准化测试等其他情境中）。
考点： although引导的让步状语从句是考研高频考点，注意although和but不能同时使用（但这里but连接的是更大的上下文逻辑）。
The prevalence of tech in schools owes less to rigorous evidence than aggressive marketing.
结构分析：
主语：The prevalence of tech in schools（学校中技术的普及）
谓语：owes less to... than...（与其说归功于...不如说归功于...）
考点： owe...to... 是"将...归因于..."的意思；less...than... 结构表示"与其说...不如说..."。

✏️ 阅读理解练习

1. 【主旨大意题】What is the main idea of the passage?
A. Education technology companies use aggressive marketing to sell their products to schools.
B. Despite huge profits, education technology rarely improves learning and often harms it.
C. McPherson Middle School successfully abandoned digital programmes and returned to traditional teaching.
D. Teachers should receive more training on how to use educational technology effectively.
正确答案：B
解析：文章开篇以McPherson中学的案例引出主题，随后用大量研究数据说明教育科技对学习效果的有限影响甚至负面作用，最后引用Bill Gates和Emily Cherkin的评论强化观点。A、C、D都是文章提及的细节，但B项最能概括全文主旨。
2. 【细节理解题】According to Rebecca Silverman's meta-analysis, early-literacy tech interventions ______.
A. significantly improved students' performance on standardised tests
B. delivered at best marginal gains, with most having little or no effect
C. were only effective for students with learning disabilities
D. proved more cost-effective than traditional teaching methods
正确答案：B
解析：根据文章第四段，"A 2024 meta-analysis... found the studies described programmes that delivered at best only marginal gains on standardised tests. The majority had little effect, no effect or harmful ones." 明确说明至多只有边际收益，大多数无效甚至有害。
3. 【推理判断题】What can be inferred from Bill Gates' remark and Emily Cherkin's comment in the last paragraph?
A. Education technology will be completely eliminated in the near future.
B. Investing money in teachers might be more valuable than spending it on ed tech.
C. It takes at least two decades to evaluate the true impact of education technology.
D. Parents should have the final say in deciding whether to use technology in schools.
正确答案：B
解析：文章最后引用Bill Gates的话说明十多年已经过去，答案已经很清楚；接着用Emily Cherkin的反问"Imagine if all that money had gone into teachers instead"暗示将资金投入教师可能比投资教育科技更有价值。这是一种委婉的批评和替代建议。
