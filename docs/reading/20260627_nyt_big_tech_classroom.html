1. 主旨大意题：What is the main idea of this article?

A. Schools should ban all electronic devices to protect students.
B. Tech companies deliberately targeted students during school hours, causing educational disruption.
C. Parents are primarily responsible for children's social media addiction.
D. Google Chromebooks are the best educational tools for students.
正确答案：B
文章核心围绕科技巨头（Meta、Snap、TikTok、Google）如何通过内部策略在上课时间吸引学生注意力，导致课堂教育受到严重干扰。文章引用了内部文件、法庭记录和多位教育工作者的观点，揭示了科技公司对学生群体的刻意追求。A选项过于绝对，C选项与文章主旨不符，D选项与文章批评Chromebook被滥用的观点相悖。

2. 推理判断题：What can be inferred from Google's internal tension mentioned in the article?

A. Google's education team completely controlled the company's strategy.
B. There was a conflict between educational goals and business interests within Google.
C. Google employees were unanimously against YouTube expansion.
D. Google decided to stop promoting YouTube in schools.
正确答案：B
文章明确提到"Tension emerged inside the company itself"（公司内部出现了紧张关系），教育团队推广教学内容，而其他员工则专注于增加YouTube观看量。这直接表明谷歌内部存在教育目标与商业利益之间的冲突。A选项与事实相反，C选项的"unanimously"（一致地）过于绝对，D选项在文中没有提及。

3. 词义猜测题：The word "drifted" in Paragraph 4 probably means ______.

A. moved gradually away from something
B. sailed quickly across the ocean
C. changed suddenly and dramatically
D. studied carefully and attentively
正确答案：A
"drifted"出现在"students drifted from schoolwork into unrelated content"这一语境中，描述学生从课业"漂移"到无关内容的过程。"drift"本义是"漂流；漂移"，引申为"逐渐偏离"。A选项"moved gradually away from something"（逐渐远离某物）最准确地捕捉了这一含义。B选项过于字面化，C选项的"suddenly"与drift的渐进含义矛盾，D选项与文意完全相反。
