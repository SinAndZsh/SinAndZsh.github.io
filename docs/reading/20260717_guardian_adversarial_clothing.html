🔍 长难句解析

Companies have started incorporating "adversarial patterns" in their garments — carefully designed arrangements of shapes, colours and repeated motifs said to exploit weaknesses in some computer vision systems.
【结构分析】主句为"Companies have started incorporating 'adversarial patterns' in their garments"；破折号后为同位语，解释 adversarial patterns 的具体内容，其中"said to exploit weaknesses..."为过去分词短语作后置定语，修饰 arrangements。【句意】各公司已开始将"对抗性图案"融入服装——这些精心设计的形状、色彩和重复图案组合，据称能够利用某些计算机视觉系统的弱点。
Evidence of misuse and that black and Asian people are more likely to be incorrectly identified than white people has led to increasing public concern.
【结构分析】主语为"Evidence of misuse and that..."，其中 and 连接两个并列的 of 宾语：一为名词 misuse，二为 that 引导的同位语从句"that black and Asian people are more likely..."；谓语为"has led to"；宾语为"increasing public concern"。【句意】关于人脸识别技术被滥用的证据，以及黑人和亚裔比白人更容易被错误识别的现实，已经引发了公众日益增长的担忧。
"If such clothing genuinely proved effective, it could get political very quickly," he said. "Then this type of clothing could find itself banned."
【结构分析】第一句为 if 引导的非真实条件句（与现在事实相反或将来可能性），主句用"could + 动词原形"；第二句中"find itself banned"为"find + 宾语 + 过去分词"结构，表示"发现自己处于被禁止的状态"。【句意】"如果这种服装真的被证明有效，它可能很快就会变得政治化，"他说，"届时这类服装可能会发现自己被禁止。"

✅ 阅读理解练习

1. 【主旨大意题】What is the main idea of the passage?
  1. British police should stop using facial recognition technology immediately.
  2. Adversarial clothing is becoming a fashionable and political response to the spread of facial recognition surveillance.
  3. Generative AI has made facial recognition systems perfectly accurate for everyone.
  4. High-street fashion brands are the main driving force behind anti-surveillance design.
正确答案：B
解析：文章围绕人脸识别技术普及背景下兴起的"对抗性服装"展开，介绍其设计原理、代表品牌、社会文化意义及潜在局限与政治风险。B 项准确概括全文主旨。A 项"立即停止"过于绝对，文中仅讨论监管需求；C 项与文中"黑人和亚裔更容易被误识"矛盾；D 项将主流品牌说成主要推动力，与文中"先锋品牌/亚文化"的表述不符。
2. 【细节理解题】According to the passage, how does adversarial clothing mainly work?
  1. It covers the wearer's face completely like a mask.
  2. It uses special patterns to confuse facial recognition algorithms.
  3. It blocks all cameras within a five-metre radius.
  4. It sends electronic signals to disable surveillance devices.
正确答案：B
解析：文章第三段明确指出，对抗性服装"works by confusing the algorithm itself"，并通过特殊图案让软件 misread what it sees。A 项混淆了对抗性服装与传统面罩；C、D 两项文中均未提及。
3. 【推理判断题】What can be inferred about the future of adversarial clothing from the passage?
  1. It will soon replace traditional CCTV systems entirely.
  2. Its effectiveness is guaranteed in all environments and against all systems.
  3. Its success may trigger government restrictions or bans.
  4. It has already become completely mainstream in British high streets.
正确答案：C
解析：文章倒数第二段引用 Nick Tidball 的观点："If such clothing genuinely proved effective, it could get political very quickly. Then this type of clothing could find itself banned." 由此可推知 C 正确。A 项将服装与监控系统对立，逻辑错误；B 项与 Bell 的 caution "effectiveness depends on the surveillance system" 矛盾；D 项"已完全主流化"与文中"on the cusp of going mainstream"不符。

  • 积累科技/社会类话题词汇：surveillance、algorithm、biometrics、facial recognition、adversarial、mainstream 等为近年考研英语一常见话题词，建议结合本文语境成组记忆。
  • 关注对比与转折：文中"Unlike...""Yet...""However...""Still..."等信号词引出作者真正强调的内容，也是正确选项的常出处。
